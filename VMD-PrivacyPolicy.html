VMD1.0 Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to VMD1.0. We take your privacy seriously. VMD1.0 is a local utility for von Mises circular distribution calculations, including probability density (PDF), cumulative distribution (CDF), polar visualizations, random sampling, maximum-likelihood fitting, and saved computation history. Before using the app, please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By tapping "I Agree", you confirm that you have read and accepted this policy.

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. VMD1.0 does not provide financial advice, trading services, brokerage, or investment recommendations. It is a pure local statistics and visualization tool.

2. Information We Collect

VMD1.0 works on your device and does not require you to create an account. We do not collect personal information from you over the internet. The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Distribution parameters you enter, such as mean direction (μ), concentration (κ), and related inputs;
  • Computation results, including PDF/CDF sample points, circular statistics, confidence arcs, histograms, and angular samples generated or imported on your device;
  • Saved computation history records, labels, timestamps, and comparison selections;
  • App preferences and consent status, such as whether you have accepted this Privacy Policy.

When you choose to use Take Photo or Choose Photo, the app accesses your camera or photo library only at your request. Photos are used on-device to let you mark directional angles. VMD1.0 does not upload your photos or camera captures to our servers. Imported images are processed locally for angle marking and are not retained as attachments in your saved runs unless you explicitly save related computation data separately.

We do not collect payment information, government ID numbers, precise location data, contacts, advertising identifiers for tracking, or analytics profiles.

3. How We Use Information

We use information stored on your device solely to provide and improve VMD1.0 features, including:

  • Performing von Mises distribution calculations and visualizations;
  • Generating, displaying, and fitting angular samples;
  • Marking directions on photos you import or capture;
  • Maintaining your local history list, detail views, and two-run comparisons;
  • Exporting or importing JSON history files when you choose to do so.

We do not sell your personal information or use it for purposes unrelated to the app without your consent.

4. Storage and Protection

Your data is stored locally on your device using iOS app sandbox storage mechanisms. Computation history and preferences remain on your device until you delete individual records, clear all data from Settings, export and remove copies yourself, or uninstall VMD1.0.

We take reasonable measures to help protect information on your device. However, no electronic storage method is completely secure. Please protect your device with a passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not transmit your calculation inputs, results, photos, or history to our servers. We may share information only when:

  • You have given explicit consent, such as when you export JSON files and choose where to save or send them;
  • Required by applicable law or a competent authority;
  • Necessary to protect the legitimate rights and interests of you, us, or other users.

When you first launch the app, it may load this Privacy Policy over the internet from our hosted policy page.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Export your computation history as JSON from Settings;
  • Import JSON files to merge or replace local history when you choose;
  • Delete individual history records in the History tab;
  • Clear all local history from Settings;
  • Decline camera or photo access and still use core calculation features that do not require media access;
  • Uninstall the app to remove locally stored data from your device.

To ask questions or request assistance, please contact us as described in Section 9.

7. Protection of Minors

VMD1.0 is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a minor, please read this policy with your guardian and use the app only with their consent. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version. Continued use of VMD1.0 after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
